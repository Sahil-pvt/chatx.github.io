What is ChatX ?

ChatX Is An Awesome Chatting App Made With Flutter


Want To Have A Casual Conversation, Make New Friends, Or Even Find That Special Someone ? Would You Like Feature-rich And User Friendly App For That?


ChatX Is Created With The Purpose To Provide A Platform For People To Connect And Communicate With One Another In Real-Time, Regardless Of Their Location And To Socialize People Online While Ensuring Their Safety And Privacy. Streaming Experience On Your Smartphone Without Annoying Ads And Very Minimal Interface


The Application Is Open Source And You Can Check On It At GitHub
